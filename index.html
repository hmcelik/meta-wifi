META-WIFI routers are hardware devices that provide encrypted data and bandwidth supply. These routers are the termination point for data encryption.

META-WIFI Hotspot

META-WIFI hotspots are physical locations that provide wireless coverage. These are built with commodity open-standard components w/o centralized control.

FAQ

Q1: What is the META-WIFI Network all about?

It’s all about letting people be connected on the web in a more convenient, affordable, and safe way. The META-WIFI network is a decentralized wireless mesh network that enables devices to connect with hotspots within the area. Enterprises can also monetize their hotspots by running ads and promotions.

Q2: Who will benefit from META-WIFI?

Basically anyone! If you are always on-the-go or you want reliable Internet access when you are outdoors, META-WIFI platform is right for you. You can earn WIFI tokens as you connect to any META-WIFI-powered hotspots. Moreover, value-added services is made available for interested enterprises and advertisers.

Q3: Where can I use WIFI tokens?

WIFI tokens serve as the virtual payment currency within the META-WIFI platform. It can be converted to points in the META-WIFI Pay Wallet. These points can be used to pay for online purchases and franchise stores or can be directly withdrawn as fiat money or cash. Moreover, WIFI can be traded once listed.

Q4: What type of consensus protocol does META-WIFI network use?

We have the Proof-of-Work (PoW) protocol as the META-WIFI platform is initially based in Ethereum. Once the need arises, its mainnet will be developed and launched. In addition, a new kind of proof called Proof-of-Coverage (PoC) ensures that the decentralized network is maintained and functioning properly.
